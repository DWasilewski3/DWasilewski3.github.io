About the Decathlon

The decathlon is a combined event in athletics consisting of ten track and field events. These events are spread over two days, and the athlete scores points based on their performance in each discipline.

Qualifications for Team USA

To qualify for Team USA in the decathlon, athletes must meet specific performance standards and demonstrate excellence in the various events. Visit the official Team USA website for more details.
