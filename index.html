About the Decathlon

The decathlon is a combined event in athletics consisting of ten track and field events. These events are spread over two days, and the athlete scores points based on their performance in each discipline. The events include sprinting, jumping, throwing, and distance running.

How Points Work in the Decathlon

The decathlon scoring system assigns points to an athlete's performance in each event. The better the performance, the more points are earned. The total points from all events determine the overall winner. The world record in the decathlon is held by Ashton Eaton with a total of 9045 points.
